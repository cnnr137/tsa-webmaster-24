Save up to $3,200 a year on your federal income taxes for making energy effciency improvements to your home. You can claim 30% of project expenses unless otherwise stated! There is a total limit of $1,200 for any combination of home envelope improvements and $2,000 for heat pumps, heat pump water heaters, and biomass stoves/boilers. Eligibility depends on income, household size, and location.
