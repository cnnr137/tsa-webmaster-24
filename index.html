THE INITIATIVES

Three ways for YOU to convert to green energy

EnergySTAR

To find home appliances which are energy efficient, choose products which are EnergyStar certified to help you conserve energy at home!

  • Save Costs on Electricity
  • Conserve Energy
  • Clean Environment
  • Tax Credits

WaterSense

When buying products such as showerheads for your bathroom or spray sprinkler bodies for your lawns, make sure your products have the WaterSense label. This guarantees water usage while allowing excellent performances.

  • Save Water
  • Exceptional Performance
  • Cost-Effcient
  • Sustainable Environment

Combined Heat and Power

CHP technologies allows usage of combined heat and power, reducing emissions in the atmosphere. All that, right from your home!

  • Significant Fuel
  • Emission Savings
  • Affordable
  • Reliable Energy
