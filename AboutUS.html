How can we claim tax credit by installing renewable energy appliances and technologies?

You can apply by filing Form 5695 with tax to claim your tax credits. You can be eligible to claim it after installing the renewable energy system, not during purchase.

How long does it take to recoup investments in renewable energy?

To recoup investments in renewable energy systems usually takes an average of six to ten years which is dependent on various factors.

How affordable is renewable energy compared to investing in non-renewable energy?

According to the International Renewable Energy Agency, In 2022 the price of renewable sources of energy such as solar photovoltaic energy cost $0.0636 per kWh while fossil fuels cost $0.2691 per kWh. Through the years, the cost of renewable is drastically dropping. Also investing in renewable energy allows for various benefits without any external cost unlike non-renewable energy which could cause environmental damage, property damage, and health problems. Renewable energy is expected to have an amazing payback as well!

What is the process for installing residential solar panels or any other renewable energy system?

To install solar panels find a qualified professional who has the certification to do so, an industry-stand certification is received through the North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners (NABCEP). Your installer will work with you to find the appropriate size for your system and work with your energy needs. Your roof must be compatible with solar panels, and if necessary, a new roof may be needed. Before installing your solar panels, you would need permits from your locality.

What kind of maintenance is required for residential wind turbines?

For maintenance you should check with your installer. Annual maintenance includes checking and tightening bolts and electrical connections, checking machines for corrosion and guy wires for proper tension. Replace worn or old edge tape on the turbine blades and replace components such as turbine blades or bearings. Many installers usually provide programs for maintenance.

How reliable are renewable energy systems in different weather conditions?

Renewable energy systems are reliable in adverse weather conditions, even during night when there is no sunlight for solar energy systems and where there is no wind for your wind turbines. Battery storage stores power from renewable energy sources and delivers it to the grid when that energy is needed, guaranteeing energy during adverse weather conditions.
